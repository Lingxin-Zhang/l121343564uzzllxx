## Diagnosis

The Round33 data does not support the stronger interpretation that a narrow
batch-`1000` peak is a proven L1-fit effect.

Conservative statement supported by the CSVs:

- The fixed `w=14` block-LUT has a small/mid-batch throughput plateau.
- Bulk throughput is highest around batch `2000` in this run.
- Throughput drops after roughly `2000` to `3000` codewords.
- Stage timing is dominated by index calculation rather than lookup or output
  write.
- Batch `1000` is near a nominal 32 KiB active-input footprint, but batch
  `1500` and `2000` exceed that footprint and still remain as fast or faster.

Therefore the safe conclusion is "small/mid batch plateau with a later drop",
not "batch 1000 proves an L1-fit peak".
